About QuickTools

QuickTools is a free online platform designed to help students and professionals with powerful and easy-to-use tools.

Our goal is to simplify everyday tasks like calculating CGPA, building resumes, checking ATS scores, and more — all in one place.

We also provide fun and engaging tools like typing tests, reaction speed games, and memory games to improve your skills while enjoying the experience.

🚀 What We Offer

  • ✔ Resume Builder & ATS Checker
  • ✔ Academic Calculators
  • ✔ Productivity Tools
  • ✔ Fun & Skill-Based Games

🎯 Our Mission

To provide simple, fast, and free tools that make your work easier and more efficient.
